About The Author

Umang Dwivedi is an independent Indian author from Bansi, Uttar Pradesh. Passionate about mystery and suspense, he wrote and independently published his debut book, The Unsolved Mystery, at the age of 15. His writing blends suspense, interconnected storytelling and Indian themes to create an engaging reading experience. He believes every mystery should keep readers guessing until the final page and continues his journey with creativity, imagination and determination.

Featured Book

The Unsolved Mystery

A suspense-filled mystery novel consisting of five interconnected chapters. Every chapter reveals new clues while staying connected to one larger mystery. Inspired by Indian settings, this book keeps readers engaged from beginning to end.
